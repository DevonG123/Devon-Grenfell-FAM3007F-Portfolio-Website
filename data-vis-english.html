Transport & Urban Development Budget Allocation and Average Annual Household Income per Ward in Cape Town

The first interactive map layer depicts how much money from the 2017/2018 Capital Transport & Urban Development budget was allocated to each of Cape Town's wards.

The second interactive map layer depicts the average annual income per household for each of Cape Town's wards.

With the visualisation of this data we should be able to see the extent to which the City of Cape Town's distribution of this particular budget is 'pro-poor' by comparing the allocation of these funds into richer areas with the allocation into poorer areas.

You are able to view the maps of Cape Town showing the capital budget's Transport Allocation per Ward and the Average Annual Household Income per Ward either separately, OR you may choose to overlay both of the maps on top of each other by selecting both layers on the pop-out menu on the left side of the map.
